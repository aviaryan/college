About Me

Hi there !
I am Avi Aryan currently pursuing B.Tech in Information Technology in IIIT Vadodara, India. I come from Patna and I am genuinely interested in the field of Computer Science. In Class 12th, I came across AutoHotkey, a scripting language for Windows which introduced me to the world of programming.
I liked it from the start and gradually became a master at it. Along with AutoHotkey, I also learned web development and Jekyll template language. In AutoHotkey, I created many small and large projects some of them including Clipjump, Avis Bot Creator for Counter Strike and LaunchQ.
Before this, I used to spend most of my time watching cartoons and playing computer games. Currently I am trying to improve my programming skills and also plan to learn modern programming languages in the upcoming vacation as well as take a few projects in these languages.
I like pop/rap music, mostly those of Linkin Park, Eminem, Taylor Swift and One Direction. My current hobbies are scrolling through Quora, playing Counter Strike and doing some competitive programming.
